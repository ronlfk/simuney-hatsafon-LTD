Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to games and apps published by Simuney Hatsafon.

1. Who We Are

Simuney Hatsafon ("we", "our", "us") operates mobile games and applications. If you have any privacy questions, contact us at: banayanofer@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We aim to collect the minimum data needed to run and improve the game:

  • Basic technical data: device type, OS version, language, and anonymous installation identifiers for stability and functionality.
  • Anonymous usage events: broad in-game events (e.g., screen or level opens) to improve user experience.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information such as full name, precise address, or phone number.

3. Ads & Third-Party Services

Some of our games may include ads or analytics provided by third parties (for example Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, or similar services). These partners may collect data according to their own policies.

We do not sell personal information.

4. How We Use Information
  • To operate the game and fix bugs or crashes.
  • To improve gameplay, balancing, and content.
  • To measure performance in an anonymous, aggregated way.
5. Children

Our games are intended for a general audience. If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal data by mistake, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Retention & Security

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described above, and we apply reasonable safeguards to protect it.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us to ask about, correct, or request deletion of your data, or to object to non-essential processing.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
